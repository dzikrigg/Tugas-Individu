BANYUWANGI

Banyuwangi Regency is the widest district in East Java as well as being the widest in Java, with an area of ​​5,782.50 km2, or wider than Bali Island (5,636.66 km2). On the coast of Banyuwangi Regency, there is the Ketapang Port, which is the main link between Java and Bali (Gilimanuk Port). Banyuwangi is a regency that is located at the eastern end of the island of Java, which is why Banyuwangi is nicknamed Sunrise of Java. and this is where I was born. the name banyuwangi itself was born from "banyu" which means water and "wangi" which means fragrant, and this name is also taken from the legend of banyuwangi. My Favorite Destination

Red Island Beach

Red Island is the destination that I visit the most. Because here is very beautiful with views of the southern sea and big waves, so Red Island beach is very suitable as a place to surf. Here we are served with clear white sand and beautiful blue sea. And what makes it special is the small island at the end of the beach which is named the "Red Island". This place is also great spot for seeing the sunset

Ijen Mountain

Ijen Mountain has an altitude of 2,386 meters above sea level. Here we can see the beautiful landscape. And at the top of Mount Ijen we will find a beautiful active sulfur crater in blue. Mount Ijen is also known to have a rare phenomenon that is the blue fire inside the Mount Ijen crater. Mount Ijen hiking trail is not too difficult either. This place is the best destination in Banyuwangi

Telunjuk Raung Waterfall

Telunjuk Raung Waterfall offers extraordinary charm, in addition to its exotic scenery, we will be spoiled with very fresh clear water. This place is also very suitable to get into water. In the waterfall area we have been presented with a beautiful natural fort. Start from the small park at the entrance to the point of the waterfall. Where there are green hills to the rocks that make the view more perfect

Surabaya State University (UNESA)

I was in college at the State University of Surabaya (UNESA). I majored in Informatics Engineering. Now I will tell you a little about my campus. Surabaya State University (Unesa) is a state university in Surabaya, Indonesia, which was established on December 19, 1964. At its inception, Unesa was named the Surabaya Teaching and Education Institute (IKIP Surabaya). Based on Presidential Decree No. 93 of 1999, IKIP Surabaya was changed to Surabaya State University. In accordance with the decision of the senate meeting on October 12, 1998, which agreed that the name of the IKIP Surabaya post-conversion was Surabaya State University. At present Unesa has 7 faculties; 4 faculties are in the Ketintang complex: the Faculty of Engineering, the Faculty of Economics, the Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences, and the Faculty of Social Sciences. While 3 faculties are in the Lidah Wetan (LiWet) complex: the Faculty of Sports Science, the Faculty of Education as well as the Language and Arts Faculty. Meanwhile, the motto that guides this tertiary institution is "Growing with Character".
